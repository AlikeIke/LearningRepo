Kill is used to send a signal to a process

Syntax: kill signal_number processID
 Example: kill -9 45622

ps aux shows the processes running on your system

pidof cmd, Process ID of command; syntax: pidof process_name
Example pidof nano

Signal IDs: 15 = Terminate, happens by default. Terminates gracefully after cleanup
9 = Kill, ills the process, terminate without cleanup.
2 = interupt, control+C
